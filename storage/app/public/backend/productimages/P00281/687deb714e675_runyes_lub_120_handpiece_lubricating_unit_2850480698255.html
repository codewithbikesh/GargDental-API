The Runyes LUB-120 Handpiece Lubricating Unit is an essential device for dental practices, designed to automate the maintenance of dental handpieces. This unit ensures thorough cleaning and lubrication, which are crucial for the optimal functioning and longevity of dental instruments. With its straightforward operation, safety features, and cost-effectiveness, the LUB-120 is a valuable tool for maintaining dental handpieces, resulting in enhanced durability and performance.

 

Effective Cleaning and Lubrication: The unit is engineered to ensure comprehensive cleaning and oiling of handpieces, contributing to their functional efficiency and extended lifespan.

 

Simultaneous Maintenance Capability: Capable of cleaning and oiling up to four handpieces at the same time, enhancing productivity and time management in the dental clinic.

 

Rotating Gear Mechanism: Features a rotating gear that cleans and lubricates handpieces from various angles for thorough maintenance.

 

Residue Removal Function: Equipped with an air compartment that, when pressed, effectively removes any excess residue remaining in the handpiece after cleaning and lubricating.

 

Customizable Lubrication Modes: Offers the flexibility to choose from short, long, and extra-long modes for lubrication, accommodating different types of handpieces.

 

Oil Mist Filter Cotton: Minimizes the diffusion of oil mist, ensuring a clean and safe working environment within the dental clinic.
