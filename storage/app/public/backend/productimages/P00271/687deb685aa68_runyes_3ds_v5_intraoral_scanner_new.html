Runyes 3DS V5 - Intraoral Scanner New

Runyes 3DS V5 - Advanced Intraoral Scanner for Modern Dentistry.

Runyes 3DS V5 is an innovative intraoral scanner that sets new standards in digital dentistry.

Designed to provide exceptional precision, efficiency and comfort, it combines advanced 3D scanning technology with a lightweight, slim design and quiet operation.

It is the ideal tool for dentists who want to improve the quality of their services, optimize workflow and provide the highest level of patient experience.

Key Features and Benefits:

  • Light and Slim Design: Ergonomic design and low weight (190 g without cable) provide a comfortable grip and minimize hand fatigue during scanning. Slim head design allows easy access to hard-to-reach areas of the oral cavity.
  • Quiet Operation: Low noise levels during scanning increase patient and staff comfort.
  • Expanded Field of View: Enables the acquisition of more data in a single scan, resulting in a more efficient workflow.
  • High Scanning Accuracy: Provides precise 3D models necessary for planning and implementing a variety of dental procedures.
  • Increased Scanning Speed: Reduces the time it takes to scan a patient's oral cavity.
  • Gesture Sensor: Enables touchless operation, improving hygiene standards.
  • Orthodontic Simulation Feature: Allows you to visualize the potential effects of orthodontic treatment.
  • Intelligent Scanbody Matching: Facilitates precise implant treatment planning.
  • Metal Scanning Capability: Enables accurate digitization of metal prosthetic restorations.
  • Cloud Case Sharing: Share data securely and conveniently with other professionals.
  • Generating an Oral Health Report: A tool to support patient communication.
  • High Resolution Scan Area: Allows detailed data acquisition from selected areas.
  • Impression Scanning: Enables digitization of traditional impressions.
  • Edentulous Relationship Scan: A feature that aids in the precise determination of occlusal relationships in edentulous patients.
  • 3D Model Optimization: Automatically improve models after scanning.
  • Multi-Format Compatibility: Supports PLY, STL and OBJ formats.
  • Anti-fogging system: fan

Technical Specifications:

  • Scanner dimensions: 245 x 41 x 57 mm;
  • Scanner Weight: 160 g (without cable) ;
  • Scanning Accuracy: up to 10 microns;
  • Depth of view: 0-25mm;
  • Frames per second: 20;
  • Field of View: 16x14 mm;
  • Imaging Method: Continuous 3D video capture;
  • Light Source: LED;
  • True Color: YES;
  • Output Format: PLY, STL, OBJ.

Runyes 3DS V5 is the perfect combination of performance, comfort of use and advanced features that will improve the work of any modern dental practice
